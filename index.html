1. Weather station with most recent weather observation Enter ICAO code:
This API returns the most recent weather observation for the specified ICAO code. The response includes information such as temperature, humidity and wind speed.
Some examples of ICAO codes are:
KJFK (John F. Kennedy International Airport), EGLL (London Heathrow Airport), OMDB (Dubai International Airport), RJTT (Tokyo Haneda Airport).
